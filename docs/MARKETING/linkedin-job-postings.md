## Posting Tips

### Best Practices:

1. **Post as "Remote"** - Attracts global talent
2. **Use "Contract" or "Freelance"** - Sets correct expectations
3. **Include salary range** - "Varies by bounty" or "$50-$500 per contribution"
4. **Add skills tags** - React, TypeScript, Solidity, Node.js, etc.
5. **Refresh weekly** - Repost or edit to stay visible

### Suggested Posting Schedule:

- Week 1: General "Open Source Contributors" post
- Week 2: Frontend-focused post
- Week 3: Smart Contract Developer post
- Week 4: Technical Writer post
- Repeat cycle
